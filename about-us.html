WHO WE ARE

Founded in 2022 with the goal of creating a safe and fun environment for young children to learn and play the world's most watched and loved sport. We are licensed by the Swedish Professional Football League (SEF) and have many years of experience. Currently, we train a team of teenagers, but we also wanted to train children aged 5-12 to be part of their exciting journey into the world of football.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

We believe that all children deserve the opportunity to discover and express themselves, without limits, to gain confidence in themselves and in everything they try. That is why we encourage children to show not only their strengths but also their weaknesses so that we can better understand them and train them in ways that we believe will be fun and beneficial to them.

WHAT WE FOCUS ON

We focus on making sure the children have fun. For this reason, we do not create a competitive environment. We believe that a competitive environment would keep kids from developing and improving the core skills they need to become football players. We will focus on basic skills such as running, jumping, shooting, catching, and throwing.
